Our Mission

The DAB community is committed to:

  • Amplifying the voices of those who have lost family members or loved ones to drugs, as well as those who are actively working to combat drug addiction.
  • Increasing drug prevention awareness in order to reduce stigma and shame.
  • Educating community organizations on how to prevent drugs abuse and to promote healing after a drug-related death.
  • Building drug prevention collaboration among DABers and other advocacy efforts to support nationwide programs and research.

Our Vision

A world of DABers:

  • All people assert that drug abuse is bad.
  • Drug-related deaths are reduced and eventually eliminated.

Advocacy

Millions of people die every day due to drug abuse, breaking families and communities. The DAB Campaign is here to support a drug-free world.
